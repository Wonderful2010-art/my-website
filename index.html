Project 1

Laws Campus Automated Greenhouse System

Laws Campus Automated Greenhouse System is designed to optimize plant growth and cultivation by controlling environmental factors with minimal human intervention. This system utilizes various technologies and sensors to monitor and adjust key parameters such as temperature, humidity, light, and soil moisture. By automating these processes, the system ensures optimal growing conditions, leading to increased efficiency, higher yields, and better resource management. Technologies Used: Microcontrollers and Development Boards: Arduino and ESP8266 Sensors: ➢ Temperature and Humidity Sensors: DHT22 ➢ Soil Moisture Sensors: Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor ➢ Light Sensors: Photodiodes.
